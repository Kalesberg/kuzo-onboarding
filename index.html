Popular questions

How are the classes run?

Classes are hosted in small groups, live, twice a week for 90 minutes each. In the first 60 minutes, you will watch the recorded content together with your Team, followed by a 30-minute discussion facilitated by a KuzoClass moderator. Every fourth session is a live assignment completed with your group for the first 60 minutes, followed by a 30-minute facilitated discussion and feedback session. [Please note, students on the spectator plan do not participate live in the discussion portion, while Teams on the Elon Musk plan will be paired with Patrick Lee as the moderator]

How are points rewarded?

Points are awarded for attendance of classes, completion of assignments and grading from your peers.

How big are teams?

Teams are typically composed of 4-5 entrepreneurs and a moderator, however, scheduling constraints may result in slightly smaller or larger groups.

What if I am only available for small windows in the week?

We will endeavor to match you with your preferred time, although we may request you provide further availability if this is not possible.

How do I know when and what time my classes are?

After checkout, we will send you a survey to collect your time availability. Teams will then be created and class times selected which are compatible with these choices. Once allocated, you will receive a Google Calendar including all your classes, and you will also receive timely email reminders.

When will I be billed?

The first payment - $65, $95 or $180 depending on your plan - will be taken upon checkout. No further payments will be taken until after you’ve experienced your first week of scheduled classes. The remaining weekly payments will resume starting the second week of classes.
